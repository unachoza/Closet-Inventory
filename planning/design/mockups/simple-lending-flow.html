Plan: planning/epics/E4-part-deux-simple-lending.md

A loan is a status, a location, and a record — the E2 spine already carries all three (on_loan, "With {person}", LoanRecord). This design is the human layer on top: two-tap capture, a person-first lent view, and the neutral nudge that saves the friendship.

Sloane · the high-value black hole
Valentino heels "with a friend, I think" since spring. Needs the care note attached at lend time, the elapsed clock always visible, and loan history as provenance.

05 — The return
One tap, honest status
Default is dirty — borrowed clothes come back worn, mirroring the return_home → dirty rule. ⚠️ Proposes changing the current return_loan → clean transition; domain-rule change, confirm before building.

i.A loan is not a new system

Status on_loan + location "With {person}" + LoanRecord already exist (E2). Simple lending adds careNote, nudgedAt, a viewer link, and loan history — UI-first, ~5 dev-days.

ii.No account required to borrow

Owner-recorded, freeform names. If the borrower is already a Part-Une viewer, the loan links to them — so full borrow-requests (US-4.2) layer on later without rework.

iii.Duration over dates

"3 months" lands; "April 2nd" doesn't. Overdue is a soft terracotta "a while," never a red alarm — this is a lending circle between friends, not accounts receivable.

iv.Never message the borrower

All nudges leave through the owner's own share sheet, prewritten but editable. The app supplies the graceful words and the plausible deniability; the human presses send.
