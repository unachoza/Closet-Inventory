# Nothing To Wear — Product Design Principles

## 1. Calm Over Busy

Interfaces should feel breathable, spacious, and emotionally lightweight.

## 2. Insight Over Raw Data

Analytics should feel emotionally meaningful, not spreadsheet-like.

## 3. Editorial Over Corporate

The app should feel closer to a fashion publication than enterprise software.

## 4. Soft Luxury

Use restraint, texture, spacing, typography, and motion to create premium feeling rather than heavy decoration.

## 5. Familiar Patterns First

Interactions should feel intuitive before they feel innovative.

## 6. Clothing Is Emotional

The product should acknowledge identity, confidence, self-expression, and habits — not just inventory management.

## 7. Reduce Decision Fatigue

The interface should help users feel clearer and lighter after using it.
